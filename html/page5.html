Campus Kjeller

Campus

OsloMet's campus at Kjeller is located in Lillestrøm municipality, approximately 20 kilometers north-east of Oslo city centre. This campus is known for being a technological and innovative center for higher education and research. It houses faculties such as technology, arts and health sciences, and offers a wide range of study programmes.

The Kjeller campus has modern facilities, including well-equipped laboratories, teaching rooms and a library. Students benefit from an active and diverse study environment, with various student organizations and events that help create an engaging student experience.

Cafeteria

OsloMet's canteen is an important meeting point for students, staff and visitors on campus. The canteen offers a varied selection of food and drink, with options that cater for different tastes and dietary preferences. It is a pleasant place where people can take a break, socialize and enjoy a meal in a relaxed atmosphere. With a focus on quality and variety, the canteen provides an excellent place for community and cultural exchange, where students and staff from different disciplines can meet and share ideas.

Library

The library offers support for research, teaching and learning through the librarians' guidance, workshops and access to electronic databases. Students and staff have access to a varied selection of books, journals and electronic resources that support the university's academic programmes. There are also rooms for group work and individual study, as well as computers and equipment for printing and copying.

The library is an important resource for everyone associated with OsloMet. The library offers a wide range of services and activities that can help improve your studies and everyday working life.

The student city Lillestrøm

Lillestrøm, situated in the Akershus county of Norway, has gained recognition as a thriving student city, attracting students with its vibrant atmosphere and diverse cultural offerings. The city is host to several educational institutions and various vocational schools, establishing itself as an academic hub. These institutions provide students with access to modern facilities, experienced faculty, and a range of academic programs, fostering a dynamic and intellectually stimulating environment.

Cultural diversity is a hallmark of Lillestrøm, contributing to its identity as a welcoming student city. Throughout the year, the city hosts numerous cultural events, festivals, and performances, allowing students to engage with various cultural expressions. This inclusive atmosphere enhances the overall student experience and promotes a global perspective, creating an enriching backdrop for academic pursuits.
