Campus Pilestredet

The Pilestredet Campus at OsloMet metropolitan university is a vibrant and modern learning arena right in the heart of Oslo. With a central location in the city's lively centre, this campus offers a dynamic and inspiring atmosphere for students and staff.

Modern canteens

The canteen serves as a pleasant social meeting point for students and staff. With a relaxed atmosphere provides it an ideal place to meet friends, colleagues or work informally between lectures.

The libraries

Pilestredet Campus library at OsloMet is a modern knowledge arena, offers a wide selection of resources and digital access, supported by helpful staff to promote students' academic success.

Advanced computer square

The data center on the Pilestredet Campus is an innovative meeting point, equipped with modern computer terminals and technological resources, created to strengthen students' digital skills and support project work.

Well positioned

Pilestredet Campus is strategically located near public transport, which makes it easy available to students from all over the city. With good connections to trams, buses and the subway, it's easy to get there themselves to and from campus.
