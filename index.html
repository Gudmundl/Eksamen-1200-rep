Oslomet

Studie the right way!

Oslomet is a public university in Norway that offers a wide range of academic programs and research activities in various fields of study, such as health, social sciences, education, technology, art, and design. OsloMet is a diverse, inclusive, modern, and innovative university that focuses on social responsibility and sustainable development. OsloMet is a university that contributes to a more sustainable welfare state and a better society for everyone. Join us and discover your potential at OsloMet!

Faculties

Health Sciences

OsloMet boasts a vibrant hub for health science studies, encompassing diverse programs and research across its Faculty of Health Sciences. Boasting Norway's most comprehensive health and social science portfolio, it caters to various academic levels.

Educational Studies

At OsloMet's bustling Faculty of Education and International Studies, the future of learning and global understanding comes alive. The faculty educates not only the next generation of teachers, from kindergarten to upper secondary, but also delves deep.

Social Sciences

At OsloMet's bustling Faculty of Education and International Studies, the future of learning comes alive. The faculty educates not only the next generation of teachers, from kindergarten to secondary, but also delves into international development

Technology, Art and Design

OsloMet's Faculty of Social Sciences (SAM) immerses you in the complexities of human behavior and societal structures. Dive into programs like Social Work, Social Policy, and Child Welfare, tackling societal challenges and working towards positive change.

Activities and facilities

Makersp

At OsloMet, creativity doesn't just have a classroom - it has a workshop! Dive into the world of OsloMet's Makerspace, a collaborative haven for students and staff across the university. Located in both Pilestredet and the Oceanlab

Athletica

Athletica at OsloMet isn't just a gym, it's a vibrant hub for student health and well-being. Operated by SiO Athletica, it boasts six locations across Oslo, each offering a dynamic mix of facilities and activities

Cantines

Craving a delicious and convenient bite on campus? OsloMet offers a diverse range of canteens to satisfy your taste buds and budget. Whether you're at the bustling Pilestredet campus or exploring Kjeller, you'll find options to refuel between lectures.

Climbing wall

OsloMet's climbing wall isn't just a vertical challenge, it's a community hub for thrill-seekers and exercise enthusiasts alike. Located at the Pilestredet Campus, this 12-meter high wall offers routes catering to all skill levels.

Campus

Campus Pilestredet

Bustling with energy and urban vibes, OsloMet's Pilestredet Campus is Norway's most centrally located university. Immerse yourself in the historic Pilestredet Park, once home to the majestic Rikshospitalet, and explore the diverse buildings housing faculties and departments. Whether you're drawn to the cutting-edge technology labs at P48, or the iconic Ellen Gleditsch house with its lecture halls, Pilestredet offers a vibrant learning environment.

Campus Kjeller

OsloMet's Kjeller Campus offers a refreshingly different learning experience compared to the bustling heart of Pilestredet. Nestled amidst open fields and a vibrant research park, Kjeller provides a tranquil setting for focused study and collaboration. The modern glass building, bathed in natural light, houses cutting-edge laboratories and facilities for students pursuing fields like Engineering, Sustainable Energy, and Computer Science.
