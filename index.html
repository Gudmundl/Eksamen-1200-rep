OsloMet Campuses

Welcome to Oslo Metropolitan University (OsloMet), a vibrant educational institution in the heart of Norway’s capital, Oslo. With over 21,950 students, OsloMet is a diverse and inclusive community that fosters intellectual curiosity and prepares students for a world of possibilities.

Our four faculties offer a wide range of courses, from Health Sciences to Technology, Art, and Design, providing a rich and varied academic environment. Whether you’re interested in Applied Computer and Information Technology, Product Design, or Civil Engineering, OsloMet has a course for you.
